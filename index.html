FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services.

How quickly can we get started with the Standard Vault?

You can be up and running in minutes. Because our platform is entirely cloud-based, there is no complex software to install or hardware to configure. Once you register, your team gets instant access to the training vault.

Can I upgrade my plan at any time?

Absolutely. As your operations scale, you can easily transition from the Standard Vault to our Integrated System. We will seamlessly migrate your data into a live ClickUp dashboard without any downtime.

How does the AI video generation work?

We leverage AI to generate and format industry-standard safety training and toolbox talks. This ensures your workforce receives consistent, highly accurate, and engaging compliance information tailored specifically to heavy industry environments.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade authentication to ensure your company's training logs, staff profiles, and compliance data are locked down. Access is strictly controlled and only visible to authorized management and personnel.

Do you offer implementation support?

Yes. All tiers come with onboarding resources to get you started immediately. For our Integrated System and Enterprise clients, we provide hands-on configuration to ensure your specific operational workflows are perfectly mapped to your new dashboard.

What integrations are available?

Our core operational workflows are built to integrate seamlessly with ClickUp for ultimate project visibility. For Enterprise clients, we can deploy custom API connections via Zapier to link with your existing HR or ERP systems.
